# Evaluate models

Evaluate and compare different models of your vendors once they have been submitted. Navigate to the Leaderboard section of your use case, for example to the [Breast Cancer Screening Use Case](https://ai.tracebloc.io/explore/ai-breast-cancer-screening-and-image-classification?tab=leaderboard).

## The Leaderboard

Vendors are ranked by the score of their best performing model. Compare

- the final model score on your test data
- model size

![Initialize](/images/Breast-cancer-screening-use-case-the-leaderboard.avif)

The leaderboard provides insights over energy consumption, number of submissions and the remaining compute budget of each team as well.
